Application of Parts

Please note that we have done our best to have complete applications listed for each part in the catalog. However, it is always best to check a parts book to verify the application. If you are uncertain about a specific application, please email the year, model, make, and any relevant serial/engine numbers and we will try to assist you in finding the right part for your car with the resources we have on hand.

Furthermore, as we still have many parts yet to inventory, if you cannot find the part you are looking for in the catalog, email with the part wanted, year, model, make, and any relevant serial/engine numbers. We will check our current inventory and reply accordingly.

Condition of Parts

  • Most parts are NOS or NOSR unless indicated USED, REPRINT, REBUILT, or REMAN.
  • All chrome or stainless steel parts will usually have some shelf wear and can usually be buffed back to like new condition. Shelf wear items, like chrome and stainless steel, are free of dents and/or rust unless noted in the catalogs.
  • Non-painted steel parts (fenders, doors, etc.) may have a coating of surface rust, unless otherwise noted in the catalogs.
